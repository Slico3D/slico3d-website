# SLICO3D Website Vision

## Mission

SLICO3D creates practical 3D printed solutions for electricians, networking enthusiasts and workshop organization.

Designed, tested and manufactured in Bavaria.

The website is not intended to be a standalone webshop at this stage. The primary sales platform is eBay.

The website serves as:

* Brand presentation
* Product catalog
* Portfolio of completed projects
* Blog and news platform
* Entry point to the eBay store

---

## Main Goal

Primary goal:

Drive visitors to the eBay store while building trust in the SLICO3D brand.

Secondary goals:

* Showcase completed projects
* Document product development
* Publish blog articles and updates
* Generate custom project inquiries

---

## Instagram Integration

Instagram and blog should support each other.

Instagram posts may reference blog articles.

---

## Future Development

Current stage:

Brand + eBay.

Future stage:

Own webshop after reaching approximately 20–30 proven products with consistent sales.

The website architecture should allow future webshop integration without major redesign.
