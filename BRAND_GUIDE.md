# SLICO3D Brand Guide

## About

SLICO3D is a German 3D printing brand based in Bavaria.

The company develops practical and durable 3D printed solutions for electricians, networking equipment, workshop organization and custom projects.

Products are designed and manufactured in Bavaria, Germany.

---

## Brand Values

* Practical
* Reliable
* Durable
* Functional
* Honest
* Made in Bavaria

SLICO3D is not a gadget brand.

The focus is solving real problems with well designed 3D printed products.

---

## Brand Voice

Use a friendly and professional tone.

German customers should feel:

* Trust
* Quality
* Reliability
* Precision

Communication should be simple and direct.

Avoid marketing buzzwords.

Avoid exaggerated claims.

Preferred style:

"Praktische 3D-Lösungen. Durchdacht. Robust. Made in Bayern."

---

## Visual Style

Clean and modern.

Inspired by:

* Apple
* Notion
* Stripe

Use plenty of whitespace.

Avoid cluttered layouts.

Design should feel premium but approachable.

---

## Color Palette

Primary colors:

* #4fd1e0
* #5fd38d
* #f5b942

Use white backgrounds by default.

Support automatic Dark Mode based on user device preferences.

## Homepage Vision

The homepage must immediately communicate:

1. Who we are
2. What we create
3. Why visitors should trust us

Hero message:

"Praktische 3D-Lösungen. Durchdacht. Robust. Made in Bayern."

The homepage should flow naturally:

Hero Section

↓

Latest News / Blog Posts

↓

Featured Products

↓

Made in Bavaria Section

↓

Instagram Feed

↓

Contact

Avoid a single static company business card page.

The website should feel alive and regularly updated.

---

## Products

Current focus:

* WAGO Holders
* Electrical Accessories
* IKEA Skadis Accessories
* Network Equipment Mounts

Products sold on eBay should also appear on the website.

eBay remains the primary sales channel.

## Blog

Blog posts should be written in German.

Tone:

Friendly ("Du")

Topics:

* New products
* 3D printing projects
* Workshop life
* Development stories
* Printing tips
* Behind the scenes

The blog is an important part of the SLICO3D identity.

## Future Plans

* Launch dedicated webshop
* Keep blog active
