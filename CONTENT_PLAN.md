# SLICO3D Content Plan

## Purpose

The blog is not a traditional company news section.

It should show:

* Projects
* Product development
* Workshop life
* Practical experience
* Real stories behind SLICO3D

The goal is to build trust, improve SEO and create a living website.

---

## Publishing Frequency

Initial target:

1 article per week.

Future target:

2 articles per week.

Quality is more important than quantity.

---

## Content Categories

### New Products

New product releases and updates.

Examples:

* New WAGO Holder
* New Skadis Accessory
* New Network Mount

---

### Projects

Special and custom projects.

Examples:

* Der Zeitdieb
* Shenlong
* YourCharge Skadis Wall

---

### Product Development

How products are created.

Examples:

* From idea to finished product
* Why PETG instead of PLA
* Design process

---

### Workshop Life

Behind the scenes.

Examples:

* Printing failures
* Lessons learned
* Daily workshop life

## Article Structure

Each article should contain:

* Strong title
* Large image
* Introduction
* Main story
* Images
* Challenges
* Results
* Conclusion

Articles should feel authentic and personal.

Use a friendly "Du" tone.

Avoid corporate language.
